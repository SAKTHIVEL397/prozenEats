Delete Your ProzenEats Account

At ProzenEats, we value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account, here is how we handle it:

📌 What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

  • Your phone number and profile will be permanently removed
  • Order history and saved addresses will be erased
  • Access to the app will be revoked
  • Some data may be retained for legal or fraud prevention purposes

✉️ How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us from your registered email or phone number to contact@prozentech.com
  2. Include your registered phone number and mention “Delete My Account”

Once we verify your request, your account will be deleted within 3-5 business days.
